About Priya

Whole student.
Whole city.
Whole self.

"I'm passionate about how institutions can support the whole student — by design, not just by chance."

Priya brings two decades of higher education expertise and a genuine love for New York's cultural fabric to everything she does. At NCII, she helps community colleges advance redesign efforts — from guided pathways to student financial stability — with a practitioner's lens and a researcher's rigor.

In Brooklyn, she's equally at home leading a slow walk down Eastern Parkway, identifying cherry varieties at the Botanic Garden, or attending the Rat Academy "just to see what it's like." Born in California, trained at UC Berkeley and Cal Poly Pomona, now deeply rooted in Brooklyn.
